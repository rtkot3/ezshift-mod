# r3 Extension for EZShift 🗓️

![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-3.2-purple)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-green)
![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-EZShift-blue)

**r3** — is an advanced UserScript designed to automate and enhance the EZShift interface. It is tailored for employees who require quick colleague lookups, team analysis, and the ability to identify staff members working outside their primary areas of responsibility.
